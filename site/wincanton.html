Restored,

A weatherboard cottage given a second life. We held what mattered — the bullnose verandah, the leadlight, the bones — and opened the back of the house to light, garden, and a Moonah that has seen more summers than the cottage itself.

Heritage held. Living renewed. A quiet conversation between the two.
